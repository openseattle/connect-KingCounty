About Connect King County

Welcome! We are glad you're here!

Connect King County's Goal is to provide a central place for those in need and the organizations looking to help them to connect. We provide a way to find the resources you need to get back on your feet, while providing organizations a way to help you find them and to coordinate their own resources with each other, to better serve the community.

To get started, visit our home page for the main list of services. It will guide you to where you need to go.

Feel free to contact us from our contact page, as well.

We are glad to serve YOU!
